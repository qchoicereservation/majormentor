10 Smart Questions To Ask A Mentor
1. If you were where I am now, what steps would you take to jump-start your career?
2. Which professional organizations do you recommend I join?
3. What are some things that you regret not doing earlier in your career?
4. What was a career-defining moment for you?
5. Do you have any tips on how can I improve my resume?
6. What hard choices did you have to make to get where you are now?
7. How can I set and prioritize meaningful career goals?
8. Can you provide guidance on networking and building professional relationships?
9. What strategies do you use for continuous learning and professional development?
10. How do you handle difficult decisions or conflicts in the workplace?
